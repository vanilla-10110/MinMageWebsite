Swords and Spells

We do based and cool things with both swords and spells!!


Welcome to Swords & Spells, the ultimate VR action roguelite game! In this thrilling adventure, you'll embark on a dangerous quest to retrieve an ancient artefact for the king, battling epic monsters and exploring dark, treacherous dungeons along the way.

Equipped with your trusty sword and powerful spells, you'll face off against all manner of creatures, from goblins and skeletons to dragons and demons. Each enemy presents a unique challenge, requiring you to master different strategies and tactics to emerge victorious.

As you explore the dungeon, you'll collect powerful items, trinkets, and relics that will help you on your journey. Some will boost your strength and resilience, while others will enhance your spells or provide special abilities. With each new discovery, you'll become more and more powerful, unlocking new spells and abilities to aid you in battle.

But be warned: the dungeon is full of traps and treacherous terrain, and death lurks around every corner. If you fall in battle, you'll need to start over from the beginning, making each run a unique and challenging experience.

With its immersive VR graphics, fast-paced action, and endless replayability, Swords & Spells is sure to keep you coming back for more. Do you have what it takes to retrieve the ancient artefact and save the kingdom? Only time will tell. Are you ready to accept the challenge?
